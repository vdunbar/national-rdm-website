Section Summary

  • Assinging and communicating responsibilities to all members of a research lab is foundational to ensuring consitent and coherent data practices.

  • Having standardized onboarding practices for new lab members, and developing documentation for new members to refer to, helps instill data management practices at at the onset of a new members tenure.

  • Having standardized offboarding practices for lab members works to prevent data/files from getting lost or “orphaned” when a person leaves, as well as several other RDM benefits.
