Section Summary

  • When considering who should have access to the files/folders of your project, start by mapping out all of those involved and their respective roles in the project(s).

  • When determining the level of access to files and folders, the three most common levels of access are:

    • Administrator or owner: full control, can invite or remove collaborators and create or delete files.

    • Editor: read and write priviledges, possible file creation and deletion priviledges.

    • Reader: read-only.
