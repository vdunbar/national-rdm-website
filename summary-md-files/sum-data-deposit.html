Section Summary

  • Data deposit is the act of putting your data and possibly other research materials into a digital platform, or repository, after a project’s completion.

  • Depositing data has a number of benefits, including the promotion of transparency and reproducibility by sharing how research findings were reached, and accerating research by allowing others to build upong your data.

  • It may not be necessary to deposit all the data associated with a project, and it’s possible that you may only want to / be able to deposit a specific selection of data files.

  • There are a number of possible repositories to deposit your data, each with their own use cases.
