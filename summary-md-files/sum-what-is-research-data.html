Section Summary

  • Data is any material or source of potential information gathered for analysis or consideration as part of developing findings or insights on a subject at any point.

  • Research data is data used as a source to support an inquiry, research, and/or creative work, and is distinguished by intended purpose rather than form. It can include things such as: documents, databases, spreadsheets, surveys, notes, films, photographs, recordings, artifacts, or specimens.

  • Research data management is the process of implementing principles through practices, documentation, and conventions, that ensures that your data retains a level of organization, clarity, consistency, and resiliency.
